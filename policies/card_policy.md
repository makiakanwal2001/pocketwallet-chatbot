# PocketWallet Card Policy
**Document:** Card Services Policy v2  
**Effective Date:** 1 January 2026

---

## Section 1: Card Types

### 1.1 Virtual Card
- Issued instantly upon Tier 1 KYC verification
- Usable for online transactions and digital payments immediately
- Supported payment networks: Visa
- Can be added to Apple Pay, Google Pay, Samsung Pay

### 1.2 Physical Card
- Available after Tier 1 KYC verification
- Delivery time: **5–7 business days** (standard), **2–3 business days** (expedited)
- Usable at ATMs, POS terminals, and online
- Must be activated via app or USSD within **30 days** of delivery

---

## Section 2: Card Controls

### 2.1 Freezing & Unfreezing
- Customer can freeze/unfreeze card instantly via app
- Freeze blocks all transactions except recurring payments already in progress
- No fee to freeze or unfreeze

### 2.2 Transaction Limits
- Daily POS limit: **PKR 200,000**
- Daily online transaction limit: **PKR 100,000**
- Daily ATM withdrawal limit: **PKR 50,000**
- Limits can be temporarily increased via app (subject to Tier 2 KYC)

### 2.3 International Usage
- International transactions disabled by default
- Enable via app under Card Settings → International Transactions
- International POS/online limit: **USD 500/day** (equivalent)
- Notify PocketWallet before traveling to avoid fraud blocks

---

## Section 3: PIN Management

### 3.1 PIN Setup
- PIN must be set before physical card can be used at ATMs or POS
- Set PIN via app under Card Settings → Set PIN
- PIN is 4 digits and must not be sequential (e.g. 1234) or repeated digits (e.g. 1111)

### 3.2 PIN Changes
- PIN can be changed anytime via app
- After 3 consecutive wrong PIN attempts, card is automatically blocked
- Unblock via app identity verification or by calling support

### 3.3 Forgotten PIN
- Reset via app: Card Settings → Forgot PIN → Identity Verification → Set New PIN
- SMS OTP to registered number required

---

## Section 4: Lost or Stolen Card

### 4.1 Reporting
- Report immediately via app (Card Settings → Report Lost/Stolen) or call **0800-POCKET**
- Card blocked instantly upon report
- Liability for unauthorized transactions after reporting: **Zero**
- Liability for unauthorized transactions before reporting: Customer responsible for first **PKR 10,000**

### 4.2 Replacement
- Replacement card ordered automatically upon lost/stolen report
- Standard delivery: **5–7 business days**, fee PKR 750
- Expedited delivery: **2–3 business days**, fee PKR 1,050 (PKR 750 + PKR 300)
- New card number and CVV issued for security — update any saved payment methods

---

## Section 5: Card Expiry & Renewal

### 5.1 Expiry
- Cards are valid for **3 years** from date of issue
- Expiry date printed on card front (MM/YY format)

### 5.2 Renewal
- Renewal card automatically dispatched **30 days before expiry**
- No action required from customer
- Renewal is free for virtual card
- Physical card renewal fee: included in annual maintenance fee (PKR 1,000)
