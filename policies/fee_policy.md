# PocketWallet Fee Policy
**Document:** Fee Policy v5  
**Effective Date:** 1 January 2026  
**Review Date:** 1 July 2026

---

## Section 1: Domestic Transfers

### 1.1 Standard Transfer
- Transfers between PocketWallet accounts: **Free**
- Transfers to external bank accounts (IBFT): **PKR 15 per transaction**
- Maximum free IBFT transfers per month: **3**
- IBFT transfers beyond 3/month: **PKR 25 per transaction**

### 1.2 Transfer Limits
- Minimum transfer amount: **PKR 10**
- Maximum single transfer: **PKR 500,000**
- Daily transfer limit: **PKR 1,000,000**
- Monthly transfer limit: **PKR 5,000,000**

---

## Section 2: International Transfers

### 2.1 Outbound International Transfers
- Fee: **1.5% of transfer amount**
- Minimum fee: **PKR 500**
- Maximum fee: **PKR 10,000**
- Processing time: **2–5 business days**

### 2.2 Inbound Remittances
- Receiving remittances: **Free**
- Currency conversion fee: **0.5% of converted amount**
- Supported currencies: USD, GBP, EUR, AED, SAR

---

## Section 3: Card Fees

### 3.1 Virtual Card
- Issuance fee: **Free**
- Monthly maintenance: **Free**
- Card replacement: **Free**

### 3.2 Physical Card
- Issuance fee: **PKR 500** (one-time)
- Annual maintenance fee: **PKR 1,000**
- Replacement card (lost/stolen): **PKR 750**
- Expedited delivery: **PKR 300 additional**

---

## Section 4: Cash Withdrawal

### 4.1 ATM Withdrawals
- PocketWallet partner ATMs: **Free (up to 3/month)**
- PocketWallet partner ATMs beyond 3/month: **PKR 30 per withdrawal**
- Other bank ATMs (domestic): **PKR 50 per withdrawal**
- International ATM withdrawals: **PKR 300 + 2% of amount**

---

## Section 5: Account Fees

### 5.1 Account Maintenance
- Monthly maintenance fee: **Free**
- Dormancy fee (no activity for 12 months): **PKR 100/month**
- Account closure fee: **Free**
- Account statement (digital): **Free**
- Account statement (physical): **PKR 200 per statement**

### 5.2 Penalty Fees
- Failed transaction due to insufficient funds: **PKR 50 per attempt**
- Chargeback processing fee: **PKR 500 per dispute**
