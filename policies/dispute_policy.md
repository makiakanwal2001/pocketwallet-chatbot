# PocketWallet Dispute & Chargeback Policy
**Document:** Dispute Resolution Policy v4  
**Effective Date:** 1 January 2026  
**Regulatory Authority:** State Bank of Pakistan (SBP)

---

## Section 1: Types of Disputes

### 1.1 Unauthorized Transaction
- A transaction the account holder did not authorize
- Examples: card used without consent, account hacked, SIM swap fraud
- Must be reported within **120 days** of transaction date

### 1.2 Merchant Dispute
- Transaction was authorized but goods/services not received, were defective, or amount was incorrect
- Must be reported within **60 days** of transaction date

### 1.3 Duplicate Transaction
- Same transaction charged more than once by the same merchant
- Must be reported within **30 days** of transaction date

### 1.4 ATM Dispute
- Cash not dispensed but account was debited
- Must be reported within **7 days** of transaction date

---

## Section 2: How to File a Dispute

### 2.1 Filing Process
1. Navigate to the transaction in the PocketWallet app
2. Tap "Report a Problem"
3. Select dispute type from the list
4. Provide description and any supporting evidence
5. Submit — you will receive a case reference number immediately

### 2.2 Required Information
- Transaction date, amount, and merchant name
- Description of the issue
- Supporting evidence (screenshots, receipts, communication with merchant)

### 2.3 Dispute Fee
- Filing a dispute: **PKR 500** (refunded if dispute is resolved in customer's favor)

---

## Section 3: Resolution Timelines

### 3.1 Standard Timelines
- Unauthorized transaction: **5–10 business days**
- Merchant dispute: **15–30 business days**
- Duplicate transaction: **3–5 business days**
- ATM dispute: **7–14 business days**

### 3.2 Provisional Credit
- For unauthorized transaction disputes above PKR 5,000, provisional credit is issued within **3 business days** while investigation is ongoing
- Provisional credit is reversed if dispute is not resolved in customer's favor

---

## Section 4: Dispute Outcomes

### 4.1 Resolved in Customer's Favor
- Full transaction amount refunded
- PKR 500 dispute filing fee refunded
- Refund processed within **2 business days** of decision

### 4.2 Resolved Against Customer
- No refund issued
- PKR 500 dispute filing fee is forfeited
- Customer may escalate to SBP Banking Mohtasib within **30 days**

### 4.3 Escalation to SBP
- If customer is unsatisfied with PocketWallet's decision
- Contact: SBP Banking Mohtasib at www.sbp.org.pk
- PocketWallet provides full case documentation to SBP within **5 business days** of escalation request

---

## Section 5: Fraud Reporting

### 5.1 Immediate Steps
- Call PocketWallet fraud hotline: **0800-POCKET (762538)** — available 24/7
- Card will be frozen immediately upon fraud report
- New card issued within **3–5 business days**

### 5.2 SIM Swap Fraud
- Report to your mobile carrier immediately
- Report to PocketWallet within **24 hours**
- Account temporarily locked pending identity re-verification
