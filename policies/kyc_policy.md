# PocketWallet KYC Policy
**Document:** KYC & Identity Verification Policy v3  
**Effective Date:** 1 January 2026  
**Regulatory Authority:** State Bank of Pakistan (SBP)

---

## Section 1: KYC Tiers

### 1.1 Tier 0 — Unverified Account
- Available immediately upon signup
- Maximum wallet balance: **PKR 10,000**
- Maximum monthly transactions: **PKR 25,000**
- No IBFT transfers allowed
- Valid for: **7 days** (must upgrade or account is suspended)

### 1.2 Tier 1 — Basic Verified
- Requirements: Valid CNIC + selfie verification
- Maximum wallet balance: **PKR 100,000**
- Maximum monthly transactions: **PKR 200,000**
- IBFT transfers allowed
- Processing time: **Instant to 24 hours**

### 1.3 Tier 2 — Full Verified
- Requirements: Tier 1 + proof of income or bank statement (last 3 months)
- Maximum wallet balance: **PKR 1,000,000**
- Maximum monthly transactions: **PKR 5,000,000**
- International transfers unlocked
- Processing time: **24–72 hours**

---

## Section 2: Required Documents

### 2.1 CNIC Verification
- Both sides of CNIC must be uploaded
- CNIC must not be expired
- Image must be clear, unobstructed, and in color
- Accepted formats: JPG, PNG, PDF (max 5MB per file)
- CNIC number format: XXXXX-XXXXXXX-X (13 digits)

### 2.2 Selfie Verification
- Must match CNIC photo
- No hats, sunglasses, or face coverings
- Well-lit, front-facing photo required
- Live selfie (liveness check) required — screenshots not accepted

### 2.3 Proof of Income (Tier 2 only)
- Accepted: Bank statement, salary slip, tax return (NTN certificate)
- Must be dated within last 90 days
- Must show full name matching CNIC

---

## Section 3: KYC Rejection & Appeals

### 3.1 Common Rejection Reasons
- Blurry or cropped CNIC image
- CNIC expired
- Selfie does not match CNIC photo
- Name mismatch between documents
- Document appears tampered or edited

### 3.2 Appeals Process
- Customer may resubmit corrected documents within **14 days** of rejection
- After 3 failed attempts, account is flagged for manual review
- Manual review takes **3–5 business days**
- Customer is notified via email and in-app notification

---

## Section 4: AML & Compliance

### 4.1 Enhanced Due Diligence (EDD)
- Triggered for transactions above PKR 500,000
- Triggered for accounts flagged by SBP watchlist
- Customer may be asked for source of funds documentation
- EDD processing time: **3–7 business days**

### 4.2 Account Freezing
- PocketWallet reserves the right to freeze accounts under active AML investigation
- Customer is notified within 24 hours of freeze
- Freeze duration: up to **30 days** pending investigation outcome
- Customer may contact compliance@pocketwallet.pk for queries
